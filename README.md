# Prime Wealth Horizon – Business Consulting & Affiliate Platform

## Overview

Prime Wealth Horizon is a business consulting and growth-focused digital platform built to help the company present its services professionally, improve online credibility, and create structured customer acquisition channels.

The platform combines corporate service presentation, enquiry workflows, and affiliate onboarding into a unified digital experience designed for clarity, trust, and conversion.

## My Role

**Full-Stack Developer**

I led the end-to-end development of the platform, handling both frontend implementation and backend functionality.

My work covered product structure, service presentation, enquiry workflows, account registration flows, and the overall digital experience.

## Business Problem

The business needed a modern digital platform to:

- Present consulting services professionally
- Improve online visibility and credibility
- Support customer enquiries and lead generation
- Enable affiliate registration and onboarding
- Create a scalable digital foundation for business growth

## Solution Delivered

I designed and developed a full-stack business platform that gives visitors a clear understanding of the company’s offerings while guiding them toward action.

The platform was built around three primary user goals:

- Understanding the company’s services
- Making business enquiries
- Registering as an affiliate
